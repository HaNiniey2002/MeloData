From Song Attributes to Brain Response

Music is more than sound. It is a combination of measurable attributes such as energy, tempo, and emotional tone. These attributes influence how the brain reacts, shaping our mood, focus, and physical responses.

How Song Attributes Affect the Brain

Each song contains hidden numerical characteristics that influence how we feel, move, and think. Understanding these attributes helps explain why music can energize, relax, or emotionally move us.

Valence

Indicates how positive or negative a song feels, influencing happiness and emotional mood.

Energy

Measures intensity and activity, affecting alertness, excitement, and motivation.

Tempo

Represents the speed of a song and can influence heart rate and physical movement.

Danceability

Shows how suitable a song is for movement, triggering coordination and body response.

Loudness

Reflects sound intensity, stimulating attention and emotional arousal.
